Future Vision — Phase 2 Highlight

"Hidden Notes" —
Pokémon GO for music.

Rare musical fragments hidden at real-world locations across Japan. Walk there, collect them, incorporate them into your compositions. A global treasure hunt powered by sound.

🗺️ How it works

Rare notes seeded citywide
Cadenz hides rare musical fragments at shrines, parks, landmarks. New drops weekly.
Walk to unlock
GPS proximity unlocks the note. It plays through your headphones in real-time — you hear the melody as you arrive.
Compose with your finds
Collected notes become building blocks. Use them in your next composition for a unique sound only you own.
Trade & collaborate
Gift notes to friends. Rare "Legendary" notes found only during festivals — cherry blossom, O-bon, New Year's.

🎯 Why it works in Japan

◆

Proven template: DQ Walk drives 30M+ users to walk specific routes. Notes = quests.

◆

Seasonal events: Japan's calendar is event-driven. Exclusive notes at Hanami = massive FOMO.

◆

Tourism tie-in: JR / tourism boards can sponsor note drops at destinations to drive foot traffic.

◆

Brand drops: ASICS drops exclusive "Marathon Note" at race finish lines. Bandai drops anime themes.

🎼 Orchestra Mode

Multiple runners, same route, different instruments. Each runner's path adds a layer (piano, violin, drums, synth), composing together in real time.

🎹 Runner A = Piano 🎻 Runner B = Violin 🥁 Runner C = Drums
